Why TPnD Matters

In today’s AI-driven, cloud-first world, simply having access to Microsoft products and platforms isn’t enough to stay competitive. You need strategic guidance, hands-on expertise, and a dedicated advisor to help you navigate complexities in presales, solution design, and rapid deployment. That’s where Technical Presales and Deployment (TPnD) comes in.

TPnD is a global Microsoft organization that empowers Microsoft Partners with the resources, knowledge, and tools to plan, build, sell, and grow high-value solutions. Whether you’re brand new to Azure, Microsoft 365, or Dynamics 365, or you’re looking to integrate advanced AI or IoT workloads, our Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs) are ready to support your journey from end to end.

Who We Are and What We Do

Our Mission

  • Empower Partners: Equip you with the capabilities to win deals, deliver impactful deployments, and stand out in a dynamic market.
  • Accelerate Time-to-Market: Provide hands-on guidance and best practices to reduce risk, streamline complex projects, and expedite go-live milestones—especially critical for emerging AI scenarios.
  • Enable Ongoing Innovation: Emphasize knowledge transfer so you can continually innovate, modernize legacy systems, and drive productivity across cloud, hybrid, or remote environments.

The Role of Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs)

Technical Presales

Develop solution demos—highlighting AI, analytics, and cloud benefits—to resonate with customer needs and address common objections.

Solution Architecture

Assess existing environments, propose target architectures, and guide you through proofs-of-concept for cloud migrations, AI enhancements, or app modernization.

Deployment Assistance

Share best practices for initial setup, networking, security, and performance, ensuring your production rollout is stable, compliant, and scalable.

Post-Deployment Optimization

Offer insights on performance tuning, security hardening, compliance, and next-generation innovations—such as advanced analytics, machine learning, or cognitive services.

The Six Core Solution Areas

TPnD engagements span all major Microsoft solution domains, enabling you to tackle projects of any scale or scope:

  • Azure Infrastructure: Modernize legacy servers, enable hybrid scenarios, and deploy container solutions (e.g., Azure Kubernetes Service). Implement Azure-native monitoring and governance (Azure Monitor, Azure Policy).
  • Data & AI (Azure): Modernize data platforms with Azure SQL, Azure Synapse, or Data Lake. Implement advanced analytics, machine learning, or cognitive services for intelligent applications.
  • Digital & App Innovation (Azure): Develop cloud-native apps using microservices, serverless architectures, and DevOps. Enrich solutions with AI capabilities and publish to the Microsoft commercial marketplace.
  • Security: Protect identities, endpoints, and data with Microsoft Entra (Azure AD), Microsoft Defender, and Zero Trust architecture. Utilize Microsoft Sentinel for AI-assisted threat detection and automated response.
  • Modern Work: Deploy Microsoft 365, Teams, SharePoint, and AI-driven tools like Microsoft 365 Copilot to enhance collaboration. Establish governance, compliance, and user adoption best practices.
  • Business Applications: Elevate customer engagement with Dynamics 365 (Sales, Customer Service, Marketing) and the Power Platform. Embed AI for predictive insights and personalized experiences (e.g., Microsoft Sales Copilot).

Our Engagement Model: Plan – Build – Sell – Grow

We tailor each engagement according to your needs, following a four-phase approach:

1. Plan

  • Discovery & Assessment: Evaluate your current environment—on-prem, cloud, or hybrid—to identify technical gaps and AI opportunities.
  • High-Level Architecture: Determine the best approach (rehost, refactor, rearchitect, or greenfield) and align solution areas (infrastructure, data, AI) with your business goals.
  • Roadmap: Create a clear project roadmap so all stakeholders understand the project scope, timeline, and desired outcomes.

Example: A mid-sized ISV wants to migrate a legacy .NET app to Azure while integrating AI. After assessing on-prem VMs and databases, we outline a plan to rehost certain components and refactor others for containers, detailing how Azure Cognitive Services can enhance the user experience.

2. Build

  • Technical Architecture & Deployment: Our PTCs guide best practices around networking, security, identity, and data—ensuring a future-proof environment.
  • Proof-of-Concept & Pilot: Validate key workloads, AI prototypes, or performance metrics in a controlled environment before scaling up.
  • Knowledge Transfer: We coach your teams on resource management, DevOps processes, and monitoring so you’re self-sufficient after the engagement.

Example: You modernize a data analytics platform with Azure Synapse, Data Lake, and Power BI. A small proof-of-concept with historical sales data paves the way for full-scale AI-driven forecasting.

3. Sell

  • Technical Presales Demos: Showcase real-world scenarios—like AI-based image recognition—that directly address your customers’ business challenges.
  • Competitive Positioning & Objection Handling: Leverage our deep Microsoft expertise to counter concerns about solution complexity or cost.
  • ROI/TCO Analysis: Develop robust, data-driven business cases to illustrate the benefits of cloud transformation, AI adoption, or workload consolidation.

Example: A partner competes to deploy Microsoft 365 for a global manufacturing client. We help create a Teams + Microsoft 365 Copilot demo that emphasizes streamlined workflow, AI-assisted productivity, and reduced IT overhead.

4. Grow

  • Post-Deployment Optimization: Adopt continuous best practices for performance, scalability, governance, and compliance—crucial for expanding AI workloads.
  • Advanced Feature Adoption: Explore additional services (machine learning, data analytics, IoT) to drive ongoing innovation and revenue growth.
  • Co-Sell & Marketplace Alignment: If you’re a Microsoft partner, we can help align your solution for co-sell and commercial marketplace exposure.

Example: After migrating to Azure, a retailer wants to integrate IoT sensors for predictive maintenance. We help with Azure Stream Analytics and Azure Machine Learning for real-time anomaly detection, turning initial success into a broader advanced solution offering.
