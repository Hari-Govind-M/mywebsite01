That’s where Technical Presales and Deployment (TPnD) comes in.

Whether you’re brand new to Azure, Microsoft 365, or Dynamics 365, or you’re looking to integrate advanced AI or IoT workloads, our Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs) are ready to support your journey from end to end.

The Role of Partner Technical Consultants (PTCs)

Technical Presales

Develop solution demos—highlighting AI, analytics, and cloud benefits—to resonate with customer needs and address common objections.

Solution Architecture

Assess existing environments, propose target architectures, and guide you through proofs-of-concept for cloud migrations, AI enhancements, or app modernization.

Deployment Assistance

Share best practices for initial setup, networking, security, and performance, ensuring your production rollout is stable, compliant, and scalable.

Post-Deployment Optimization

Offer insights on performance tuning, security hardening, compliance, and next-generation innovations—such as advanced analytics, machine learning, or cognitive services.

The Six Core Solution Areas

Azure Infrastructure

Modernize legacy servers, enable hybrid scenarios, and deploy container solutions (e.g., Azure Kubernetes Service). Implement Azure-native monitoring and governance (Azure Monitor, Azure Policy).

Data & AI (Azure)

Modernize data platforms with Azure SQL, Azure Synapse, or Data Lake. Implement advanced analytics, machine learning, or cognitive services for intelligent applications.

Digital & App Innovation (Azure)

Develop cloud-native apps using microservices, serverless architectures, and DevOps. Enrich solutions with AI capabilities and publish to the Microsoft commercial marketplace.

Security

Protect identities, endpoints, and data with Microsoft Entra (Azure AD), Microsoft Defender, and Zero Trust architecture. Utilize Microsoft Sentinel for AI-assisted threat detection and automated response.

Modern Work

Deploy Microsoft 365, Teams, SharePoint, and AI-driven tools like Microsoft 365 Copilot to enhance collaboration. Establish governance, compliance, and user adoption best practices.

Business Applications

Elevate customer engagement with Dynamics 365 (Sales, Customer Service, Marketing) and the Power Platform. Embed AI for predictive insights and personalized experiences (e.g., Microsoft Sales Copilot).

Understanding Eligibility & Benefits

TPnD benefits are available to Microsoft partners in the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program, including Solutions Partner designation, Partner Success Core or Expanded benefits, Legacy Gold/Silver status, and Microsoft Action Pack (MAPS).

Note: Partners not holding one of the designations above are generally ineligible for TPnD services. In scenarios where a customer or non-eligible reseller joins the same call, the eligible partner must still be the primary beneficiary of the service.

How to Submit a TPnD Services Request

  1. Sign in to Partner Center and ensure your organization is enrolled in the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program (Solutions Partner, Legacy Gold/Silver, Action Pack, etc.).
  2. Select “Benefits,” then choose Technical Presales & Deployment (TPnD).
  3. Pick your Advisory Hours benefit (if you have multiple, select the right bucket).
  4. Create a case by providing a title, description, relevant product area (Azure, Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365, etc.), and your full international dial code with preferred working hours.
  5. Submit your request and receive a confirmation page and email with your case number.

Important: If your account is only associated with a reseller/CSP program and not with the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program, you may see an error indicating insufficient permissions. Confirm you’ve signed in with the correct account.
