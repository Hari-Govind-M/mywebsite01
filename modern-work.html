Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can TPD help me with both presales demos and actual deployment guidance?

A: Yes. TPD provides end‐to‐end technical presales and deployment assistance— from solution envisioning to best practices for production rollout.

Q2: Do I need an existing Microsoft 365 tenant to work with TPD?

A: If you’re in a presales scenario, TPD can direct you to demo environments or trial tenants. For deployment, you’ll need at least one tenant linked to your partner org or customer.

Q3: What about ongoing support or break‐fix after deployment?

A: TPD is for advisory and deployment, not continuous support. Consider Premier Support or Advanced Support for Partners for production break‐fix.

Q4: How does TPD help accelerate user adoption?

A: TPD consultants share adoption best practices, user onboarding tips, training resources, and communication strategies—crucial for maximizing your Modern Work ROI.

Q5: Is Microsoft Copilot for Microsoft 365 generally available, or do I need special access?

A: Availability/licensing for Microsoft 365 Copilot may vary by region and partner status. TPD will provide the latest details and enrollment guidance.
